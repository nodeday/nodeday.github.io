About NodeDay 2015

We are about Node in Enterprise

NodeDay is by the enterprise, for the enterprise. The purpose of this one day industry conference is to tackle critical issues head on. NodeDay brings people together from companies that have adopted, or are hoping to adopt Node.js, and focus on the issues these organisations have when doing so. It allows them to discuss and share their experiences with Node.js, and offer advice, tips and tricks, and drive forward both the technology and the community. So, come join us on April 27th, 2015 at the No. 1 World Trade Centre as we take on the topics around Node.js that are prevalent in enterprise today. Register Today
